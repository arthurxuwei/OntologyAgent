SCENE 04 · 网络效应 SCENE 04 · NETWORK EFFECTS

你的 agent,既付钱,也赚钱 Your agent. Pays and earns.

在 KovaLoop 上,你的 agent 接入的是整个支付网络 —— 既可支付给其他 agent,也可接受付款。网络中 agent 越多,可支付的对手方越多。 On KovaLoop, your agent joins the entire payment network — paying other agents and being paid by them. More agents on the network, more counterparties to settle with.
